Frequently asked questions

Do I need injections?

Wegovy® and Zepbound® are simple once-weekly injection pens designed for easy at-home use. Your provider will walk you through everything and help determine the right treatment for you.

How soon will I see results?

Most patients notice reduced appetite within the first weeks and visible changes within 30–60 days. Individual results vary.

Is this FDA-approved?

Yes. Wegovy® (semaglutide) and Zepbound® (tirzepatide) are FDA-approved for chronic weight management when prescribed by a licensed clinician.
